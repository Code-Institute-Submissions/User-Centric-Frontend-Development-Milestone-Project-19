ABOUT

BACKGROUND

Ernestina is a freelance digital illustrator. In 2019, Ernestina completed the "Graphic Design Specialisation" offered by the Californian Institute of the Arts via Coursera - this has helped her to expand her portfolio and soliify her knowledge.

STYLE

Her inspiration comes from the human figure an elements in nature. You can observe a flat design with dominant gradients as well as the ocassional texture.

TOOLS

She uses both vector and pixel based tools to complete her works. Mastering the Adobe Suite (Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Dimension), Affinite Designer and Procreate for iPad software.
